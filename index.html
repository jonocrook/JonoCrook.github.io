Professional Summary

  • I am currently studying at Australian Catholic University to become a Technology and Applied Studies (TAS) teacher, with a strong focus on hands-on, practical learning and student engagement. Alongside my studies, I work as a Learn to Swim Teacher at Macquarie University Aquatic Centre, where I have developed skills in communication, behaviour management, and tailoring instruction to meet the needs of diverse learners.
  • Through my university program, I have successfully completed two professional placements within the TAS faculty, where I gained experience in lesson planning, classroom management, and delivering engaging learning sequences across timber, design, and technology contexts. These placements strengthened my ability to connect curriculum content to real-world applications, while also reinforcing the importance of building positive relationships with students.

  • I am currently completing my Teaching degree, where I am developing the knowledge, skills, and professional practice required to become an effective educator. As part of my studies, I have successfully completed placements teaching TAS subjects across Stages 4, 5, and 6, gaining valuable classroom experience in lesson planning, student engagement, and differentiated instruction.
